Hardware Replacement Guide

Follow these step-by-step instructions to safely replace internal components. Use action verbs: Unplug, Remove, Insert, Secure.

RAM

Focus: Slots and insertion click

  1. Unplug the power cable and remove the battery.
  2. Open the back panel by unscrewing the cover.
  3. Locate the RAM slots on the motherboard.
  4. Remove the old RAM by pressing the side clips outward until it pops out.
  5. Insert the new RAM module at a 45° angle into the slot.
  6. Press down firmly until it clicks into place.
  7. Secure the back panel and reconnect the power.

SSD

Focus: Data storage and speed

  1. Unplug all cables and turn off the device.
  2. Remove the back cover screws with a Phillips screwdriver.
  3. Locate the SSD bay or M.2 slot.
  4. Remove the retention screw holding the SSD.
  5. Slide out the old SSD carefully.
  6. Insert the new SSD into the slot at the correct angle.
  7. Secure with the retention screw and close the panel.

Battery

Focus: Power and safety

  1. Unplug the charger and turn off the device completely.
  2. Remove the back cover carefully.
  3. Disconnect the battery connector from the motherboard.
  4. Remove any adhesive tape or screws holding the battery.
  5. Lift out the old battery — do not bend or puncture it.
  6. Insert the new battery and connect the cable.
  7. Secure the cover and test the device.

Wireless Card

Focus: Connectivity

  1. Unplug the power and remove the battery first.
  2. Open the laptop's back cover.
  3. Locate the wireless card (small card with two antenna wires).
  4. Disconnect the antenna wires gently using a plastic tool.
  5. Remove the retention screw and slide the card out.
  6. Insert the new wireless card into the M.2 or mini PCIe slot.
  7. Secure with the screw and reconnect both antenna wires.
